10. Skins

Bad news : TiEmu uses its own skins; they are not VTi compatible. Why ? Because the VTi skin format is somewhat limited and don't take care of some portability issues.
Good news : you can re-use your VTi skins !

Well, how can I use or develop TiEmu skins ? Julien Blache has developped a skin editor, called SkinEdit :


<>SkinEdit has all of the basic functions of a skin editor and some add-ons :
- import/export of VTi 2.1, VTi 2.5 and TiEmu 2.0 skin format (for all calcs: TI82..V200),
- load any image format (not restricted to JPEG). This feature depends on pixbuf loaders provided by GTK+ libraries.

Note : the TiEmu v2.0 skin format is documented. See the source archive.
